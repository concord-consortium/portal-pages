Hello Early Adopters! This page contains Geniventure support materials for teachers. To complete the requirements of the pilot program, start by taking the online course. We will step you through the game and present practical teaching tips. By the end of the course, you will be familiar with the structure of the game, the progression of concepts, the different types of challenges, and various teaching strategies.
